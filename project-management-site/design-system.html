UI specifications / UI 规范

Global versus B2B

Global official website

Expressive, visual, brand-led, product-led and route-led.

  • Dark industrial backgrounds.
  • Real product, factory, route or store imagery.
  • Top navigation follows Home, Shop Bikes, Technology, WorldTour, Manufacturing, Partners, Dealers, Support and B2B Login.
  • Homepage narrative follows trust first: Hero, partner confidence, manufacturing, WorldTour, bike solutions, dealer growth, find/apply CTA.
  • Every visual must match the exact module claim, especially technology, material and wheelset sections.
  • Hero, route sections, top-aligned proof cards and custom-bike layouts.

B2B operations

Dense, practical, workflow-led, data-led and role-led.

  • Topbar plus sidebar app shell.
  • Readable three-column portal lanes, KPI row, toolbar, table, task queue and status panels.
  • Light operational content surfaces with compact controls.
Components / 组件

Reusable interface rules

P0 Boss requirement MVP Core FR-052

Buttons: labels must describe the action; compact controls are used in B2B screens.

Tables: use `.table-wrap` for overflow; keep headers small, uppercase and sortable where useful.

Navigation: public pages use the PPT IA matrix; management pages use `.app-sidebar` and `.side-menu`.

Breadcrumbs: show breadcrumbs on second-level and deeper public pages only; top-level routes stay clean.

Imagery: image-to-copy match is mandatory; source or create a matching asset before shipping specific technical claims.

Governance: reusable visual, interaction or layout changes must update `design.md` and this page.

Design sync: accepted PPT optimization recommendations must be reflected in matching hi-fi screens, `design.md`, this page and related requirement documents in the same work cycle.
