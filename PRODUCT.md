# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

X-LAB Global serves international distributors, component and manufacturing suppliers, authorized dealers, retail stores, and performance cyclists. Visitors arrive with different intent: some need WorldTour proof, some need technology and manufacturing confidence, some need cooperation terms, and some need a direct route to product experience and purchase support.

## Product Purpose

The website explains X-LAB as a global strategic platform, not only a product catalog: race validation, technology showcase, controlled manufacturing, channel partnership, dealer success, support, and consumer purchase guidance. Success means visitors can move from trust proof to the right business or buying path without guessing which portal or contact point applies.

## Brand Personality

Technical, direct, high-pressure. The brand should feel built from the factory floor and race road at the same time: precise enough for partners, energetic enough for riders.

## Anti-references

Avoid a single brochure page that hides business paths in generic buttons. Avoid vague premium language without operational proof. Avoid treating suppliers, dealers, retail stores, and riders as one audience.

## Design Principles

- Map the chain: every major section should show where it sits in the upstream to downstream business flow.
- Prove before selling: racing, manufacturing, testing, and logistics evidence should appear before cooperation calls to action.
- Follow the PPT narrative spine: Hero declaration, partner confidence, manufacturing proof, WorldTour proof, bike solutions, dealer growth, then find/apply CTA.
- Use the public IA matrix: Home, Shop Bikes, Technology, WorldTour, Manufacturing, Partners, Dealers, Support, and B2B Login.
- Separate roles clearly: suppliers, distributors, dealers, stores, and riders need distinct routes, copy, and next steps.
- Keep the page operational: cards and links should lead to real route-level pages, not only anchors.
- Make purchase confidence visible: store service, fit, warranty, and test-ride support belong near product discovery.
- Keep design synchronized: once a PPT recommendation is accepted as direction, update the matching high-fidelity screens, `design.md`, `design-system.html`, and related requirement documents in the same work cycle.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-visible focus, reduced-motion support, responsive text that does not overflow on mobile, and clear link labels that stand alone.
